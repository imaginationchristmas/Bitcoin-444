Step 1

Knowledge is Power

Before you spend a single dollar, understand what you're getting into. This isn't another crypto hype guide — this is Bitcoin only.

The initial knowledge and understanding of Bitcoin is the first and biggest obstacle. Start by putting in just $20 and doing some research. You are not going to "get it" in one conversation or video — and that's totally normal.

If all you've done is read headlines and listen to CNBC or Bloomberg, you've been fed what those sources want you to think about BTC. Time to go deeper.

⚠️ Bitcoin only. This guide covers Bitcoin exclusively. Other cryptos — over 99.9% are literal scams, pump and dumps. No smart or institutional money takes them seriously. Sell them or swap/convert into BTC.

The Fundamentals

Why Bitcoin is Different

Here are the key properties that make Bitcoin unlike anything that came before it.

₿
100 Million Satoshis

1 Bitcoin is made up of 100 million sub-units called Satoshis (Sats). You'll interact with Bitcoin in its fractional form — think of it like pennies to a dollar.

📦
Fixed Supply: 21 Million

There will only ever be 21 million Bitcoin. Ever. This hard cap makes it the scarcest monetary asset ever created by humans.

🔗
Peer-to-Peer

No bank, no middleman, no approval needed. You send YOUR money directly to anyone, anywhere — without asking permission.

🌐
Decentralized

No single group, government, or corporation controls Bitcoin. It runs on a network of thousands of nodes worldwide.

📈
4-Year Track Record

From any date in Bitcoin's history, the price has been higher 4 years later. Every single time. No other asset can claim that.

🏅
Better Than Gold

Bitcoin has all the properties of gold — scarce, durable, fungible, divisible — but it's digital, portable, and verifiable in seconds.

🌍
No Borders

Send and receive Bitcoin anywhere in the world, 24/7/365. No bank hours. No wire fees. No geographic restrictions.

📉
Everything Gets Cheaper in BTC

When priced in Bitcoin, the cost of goods and services trends down over time. Fiat currencies inflate — Bitcoin doesn't.

Step 3

Take Custody of Your Bitcoin

When Bitcoin is on an exchange, you don't actually own it. True ownership means holding your own keys.

🔑

Your Seed Phrase is Everything

When you create a wallet, you'll receive a 12 or 24-word seed phrase — randomly generated, shown only once. This phrase is your Bitcoin. Anyone who has it can access everything. Guard it like your life depends on it, because your financial future might.

✍️
Write It Down — Legibly

Take your time. Write every word in order, spelled correctly. Store in a weather-protected safe at home. Consider laminating it.

🔒
Never Digital

Do NOT take a photo of your seed phrase. Do NOT store it online, in cloud storage, or email it to yourself. Ever.

🤐
Tell Nobody

Your seed phrase is more sacred than your Social Security number. Share it with no one. Not family, not friends, not "support agents."

🔨
Stamp Seed — Metal Backup

Stamp your seed phrase into titanium using the Stamp Seed kit. Fireproof, waterproof, and built to last generations. Use code "simply" for ~15% off.

💡 Pro Tip When setting up a new wallet: write down your seed phrase → wipe the wallet → restore it using the phrase → confirm it works → THEN add Bitcoin. This verifies you wrote the phrase correctly before any funds are at risk.
Wallet Types

Choosing Your Wallet

There are two types of wallets. Where you store depends on how much you have and how long you plan to hold.

A hot wallet is connected to the internet — convenient for quick access and small daily transactions, similar to Venmo or Cash App. Good stepping stone before committing to hardware. Not ideal for long-term storage of large amounts.

Strike

Built-in Lightning wallet — great for small, fast payments. Also where you buy sats.

Blue Wallet

Open-source, Bitcoin and Lightning support. Simple interface, widely respected.

Muun Wallet

Beginner-friendly wallet with seamless Lightning support. Easy to get started.

Aqua Wallet

Mobile-first option designed for ease of use with Lightning and on-chain support.

A cold storage wallet is completely offline — the modern equivalent of a gold vault, but accessible to everyone. Since it's never connected to the internet, it's virtually impossible for hackers to access. Treat it like your life savings: not for everyday use.

⚠️ Always buy hardware wallets DIRECTLY from the manufacturer. Never from a reseller.

Rules of the Road

Do's & Don'ts

Common mistakes and misconceptions — avoid these and you're already ahead of most people.

✓ Do This

  • Buy some Bitcoin and start educating yourself
  • DCA (dollar-cost average) — don't try to time the market
  • Take self-custody of your Bitcoin
  • Start small when learning to send BTC
  • Spread holdings across multiple wallets
  • Learn about the $5 wrench attack and practice security
  • Share the knowledge with your inner circle
  • HODL — hold long term

✗ Don't Do This

  • Talk about how much Bitcoin you have
  • Share your seed phrase with ANYONE
  • Try to trade in and out for quick profits
  • Photograph or digitally store your seed phrase
  • Buy other cryptocurrencies (they're mostly scams)
  • Sell your Bitcoin — institutional money is buying, not selling
  • Panic sell during dips
  • Put a target on your back bragging about your stack

The 4-Year Rule

"I'm going to buy at this price and sell when I make a big enough profit" — don't. You'll owe capital gains taxes, and you'll likely sell too early. There has never been a point in Bitcoin's history where it wasn't higher 4 years later.

"It takes 4 years to see significant gains and 8 years to make life-changing money."
